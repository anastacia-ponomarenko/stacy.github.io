Ave Avocado!

Delicious or healthy? With avocado, you don't have to choose.

Delicate nutty taste, nutrients indispensable for human body - what else is hidden in a green fruit?

Avocado contains:

  • a lot of carotenoids, which contribute to the preservation of youth at the cellular level.
  • highest amount of fiber of all fruits: one portion provides 40% of the daily requirement for dietary fiber.
  • a bunch of vitamins: A, B6, C, K, E, zinc, iron, potassium, magnesium, phosphorus.

Regular consumption of this food helps lower cholesterol levels and reduces the risk of atherosclerosis and cardiovascular disease.

Avocado goes well with all products, decorating the taste of any dish.

You can make salad, appetizer, soup, main course and even dessert with it.

The classic combination is avocado with lemon juice and hot pepper - it's called guacamole.

Try the classics first, and forward to experiments!
